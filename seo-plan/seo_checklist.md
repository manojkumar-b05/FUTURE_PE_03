# SEO Checklist

## On-Page SEO

- Keyword in Title
- Keyword in URL
- Keyword in Meta Title
- Keyword in Meta Description
- H1 Heading
- H2 & H3 Headings
- Image Alt Text
- Internal Links
- External Links
- Call-to-Action

---

## Content Quality

- Human-readable
- Original
- Informative
- Structured
- Engaging

---

## Local SEO

- Business Name
- Business Address
- Location Keywords
- Google Business Profile
- Customer-focused FAQs

---

## Technical SEO

- Proper heading hierarchy
- Mobile-friendly formatting
- Fast-loading content
- Easy readability
